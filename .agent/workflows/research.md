# RESEARCH COMMAND

**Purpose:** When facing complex issues requiring external knowledge, systematically research the problem.

**What RESEARCH does:**
1. **Identifies Research Need:** When internal debugging isn't sufficient
2. **Conducts Targeted Research:** Search for specific technical problems
3. **Applies Research Findings:** Implement solutions based on research results
4. **Documents the Solution:** Explain why research was needed and show before/after

**Usage:** `RESEARCH [specific-problem]` when facing technical issues needing external knowledge.
