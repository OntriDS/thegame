  Your product is technically reliable and generates steady revenue, but

    content: Cut prices and add new revenue streams to drive volume
    pros:
      - immediate revenue lift
      - makes the product accessible to more buyers
    cons:
      - erodes profit margins
      - signals low value to the market
      - does not address why users are not adopting the product
  - id: b
    content: Add more features and improve technical performance
    pros:
      - leverages existing engineering strength
      - addresses technical capability gaps
      - increases platform reliability
    cons:
      - more features rarely fix adoption issues
      - increases product complexity
      - may build capabilities users do not want
  - id: c
    content: Invest in user research and a design overhaul to boost desirability
    pros:
      - addresses the root cause of weak adoption
      - builds emotional connection with users
      - aligns the product with real user needs
    cons:
      - research takes time before revenue impact is felt
      - redesign adds short-term cost
